Hindu Malayalee Mandalam of Carolinas

About Us

Our organization had its beginnings dating back to 1999 when a few families in the Research Triangle Area (RTP) of North Carolina met to celebrate Vishu in a traditional way. With each subsequent year, more and more Malayalee Hindu families joined the fold and a formal organization named HIMAM (Hindu Malayalee Mandalam) was formed in the year 2016.

Today HIMAM carries the Malayali Hindu mantle in the Triangle area and beyond. Hindu Malayalees of the area are in relentless pursuit of making HIMAM reflect, facilitate and embody the core Kerala Hindu values and aspirations.

We are continuing to build on our foundations and nurture HIMAM to make it an organization that all Hindus can call ‘our own’. One that is vibrant and endearing to all the members and an embodiment of our rich Malayali Hindu heritage, traditions and values.

We are a Kerala Hindu Non-Profit Organization operating within the broad guidelines under section 501(C) (3) of the USA Revenue Code.

Our Vision

A vibrant community deep-rooted in Kerala Hindu traditions and values and practices Kerala Hindu observances and festivities. A community whose members imbibe values of tolerance, compassion and core Kerala Hindu ideals. A community whose members stand for each other at all times and help build an inviting place that all Kerala Hindus can call ‘Home’.

Our Mission

HIMAM will organize/help organize cultural, religious, social and educational activities and initiatives, to support other HIMAM missions or to raise funds to help fulfill HIMAM Vision and Mission. HIMAM will welcome Kerala Hindu families and those practicing Kerala Hindu religious values moving to the RTP area of NC by helping them ease their assimilation into the HIMAM community.

HIMAM will help instill awareness among the youth and less-informed about the life-moulding messages contained in Kerala Hindu ancient scriptures by engaging in activities that help imbibe such messages. HIMAM will engage with other similar minded organizations in the USA and outside to foster and advance Kerala Hindu ideals and values.

HIMAM provide platforms and opportunities for Kerala Hindus to connect, interact, care and share in a supportive and cooperative framework. HIMAM will help foster unity among its members and help channelize their collective effort in realizing HIMAM vision.
